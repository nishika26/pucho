# JN Tata Endowment Loan Scholarship

Founded in 1892 by Jamsetji Nusserwanjee Tata, this scholarship provides merit-based loan scholarships for higher education abroad.

## Eligibility Criteria

- Applicant must be below 45 years.
- Must graduate from a recognized Indian university.
- Minimum **60% marks** required.
- Must be admitted to a postgraduate program abroad.
- Students already studying overseas may also apply if at least one academic year remains.
- Past JN Tata scholars who repaid earlier loans can reapply.

## Benefits

- Loan amount awarded based on merit.

## Required Documents

- Passport photo
- Aadhaar card
- Passport
- Academic transcripts
- Statement of Purpose
- Work experience certificate (if applicable)
- Appointment letter (if applicable)
- ITR / salary slips

## Application Process

1. Visit official scholarship page.
2. Click **Apply Now**.
3. Register and login.
4. Fill application.
5. Upload documents.
6. Submit.

## Contact Details

- **Address:** Mulla House, Fort, Mumbai – 400001  
- **Email:** jnte@tatatrusts.org  
- **Phone:** (022) 6665 7774 / 7198 / 7681

# JK Tyre Shiksha Sarthi Scholarship Program

Scholarship for female students who are daughters of heavy vehicle drivers.

## Eligibility Criteria

- Open to female students.
- Must be daughter of heavy vehicle driver.
- Enrolled in UG or diploma course.
- Minimum **55% marks**.
- Family income ≤ ₹5,00,000.
- Must belong to Rajasthan, MP, Uttarakhand, Karnataka, or Tamil Nadu.

## Benefits

- Technical UG: ₹25,000  
- Non-Technical UG: ₹15,000  
- Diploma: ₹15,000  

## Required Documents

- Passport photo
- Admission proof
- Marksheet
- Fee receipts
- Income proof
- ID proof
- Bank passbook
- Parent profession proof

## Contact Details

- **Email:** jktyrescholarship@buddy4study.com  
- **Phone:** 1800-258-1100
