# Top 10 Central Government Scholarships for MBBS Students in India

---

Pursuing an MBBS degree in India is a dream for many students, but the financial burden of healthcare education can be significant. Fortunately, the Government of India offers several scholarships to help deserving students continue their education without financial stress.

Below is a curated list of the **Top 10 Central Government Scholarships for MBBS Students in India**.

---

## 1. Central Sector Scheme of Scholarship (CSSS)

**Eligibility:**
- Top 80th percentile in Class 12 board exams  
- Annual family income below ₹4.5 lakh  
- Enrolled in a recognized institution  

**Benefits:**
- ₹12,000/year for first 3 years  
- ₹20,000/year for 4th and 5th years  

---

## 2. Post-Matric Scholarship for SC Students

**Eligibility:**
- Belong to Scheduled Caste category  
- Annual family income below prescribed limit  
- Studying in a recognized institution  

**Benefits:**
- Tuition fee reimbursement  
- Maintenance allowance  

---

## 3. Post-Matric Scholarship for ST Students

**Eligibility:**
- Belong to Scheduled Tribe category  
- Studying in a recognized institution  

**Benefits:**
- Full tuition fee reimbursement  
- Maintenance allowance  

---

## 4. Merit-cum-Means Scholarship for Minority Students

**Eligibility:**
- Belong to minority communities:
  - Muslim  
  - Christian  
  - Sikh  
  - Buddhist  
  - Jain  
  - Parsi  
- Minimum 50% in previous exam  
- Annual family income below ₹2.5 lakh  

**Benefits:**
- Tuition support  
- Monthly maintenance allowance  

---

## 5. Top Class Education Scheme for SC Students

**Eligibility:**
- SC category students  
- Admission in top institutions through merit  

**Benefits:**
- Full tuition fee coverage  
- Living expenses  
- Books and stationery allowance  

---

## 6. Top Class Education Scheme for ST Students

**Eligibility:**
- ST category students  
- Admission in recognized premier institutions  

**Benefits:**
- Tuition fee reimbursement  
- Hostel and maintenance support  

---

## 7. National Fellowship and Scholarship for Higher Education of ST Students

**Eligibility:**
- ST students pursuing higher education  

**Benefits:**
- Financial assistance for education-related expenses  

---

## 8. Scholarships for Students with Disabilities

**Eligibility:**
- Students with benchmark disabilities  
- Valid disability certificate required  

**Benefits:**
- Tuition fee support  
- Maintenance allowance  

---

## 9. Prime Minister Scholarship Scheme (PMSS)

**Eligibility:**
- Wards of ex-servicemen  
- Minimum academic performance criteria  

**Benefits:**
- Monthly scholarship support  

---

## 10. AICTE Pragati / Saksham Scholarship

**Eligibility:**
- Pragati: Female students  
- Saksham: Differently-abled students  

**Benefits:**
- Financial support for tuition and academic expenses  

---

# Documents Required

Keep these documents ready while applying:

- Aadhaar card  
- NEET scorecard  
- Class 10 & 12 marksheets  
- Income certificate  
- Category certificate (if applicable)  
- Disability certificate (if applicable)  
- Admission letter  
- Bank account details  

---
