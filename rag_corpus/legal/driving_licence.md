The single most common reason a DL application gets delayed—or rejected outright—has nothing to do with how well you drove in the test. It is paperwork.

Wrong documents, mismatched names, unregistered rent agreements, photographs with off-white backgrounds—all of these cause rejections that force a second RTO visit, a new appointment slot, and occasionally a fee resubmission.

This guide covers the complete documents required for driving licence applications in India for 2026, whether you are applying for a Learner's Licence, converting to a permanent DL, renewing, replacing a lost one, or applying for an International Driving Permit.

---

# Documents Required for Driving Licence: The Three Core Categories

Regardless of application type, DL documentation in India always breaks down into three categories:

- **Proof of Age**: Confirms you meet the minimum age requirement for the vehicle class you are applying for  
- **Proof of Address**: Establishes your residential jurisdiction under the applying RTO's authority  
- **Application-specific documents**: Forms, declarations, and category-specific additions depending on the DL type  

The exact combination required varies depending on whether you are applying for:
- New Learner's Licence  
- Permanent DL  
- HMV or transport licence  
- Renewal  
- Duplicate licence  

---

# Proof of Age for Driving Licence: What RTOs Actually Accept

Any one of the following documents is sufficient as age proof.

- **Aadhaar Card** — Most widely accepted, digitally verifiable via OTP on Sarathi portal  
- **10th Standard Board Marksheet** — Accepted universally  
- **Birth Certificate** — Useful for minor applicants  
- **Passport** — Valid everywhere  
- **School or College Leaving Certificate**  
- **Transfer Certificate**  

## Most Commonly Accepted

1. Aadhaar  
2. 10th marksheet

# Valid Address Proof for Driving Licence: What RTOs Actually Accept

Accepted documents:

- **Aadhaar Card**  
- **Voter ID Card**  
- **Passport**  
- **Electricity / Water / Telephone Bill** (not older than 3 months)  
- **Bank Passbook / Bank Statement**  
- **Ration Card**  
- **LIC Policy Document**

## Is Rent Agreement Valid?

Only if it is a **Registered Rent Agreement**.

⚠️ A notarised rent agreement is usually rejected.

### Better Approach for Tenants

Update your Aadhaar address via UIDAI and use Aadhaar instead.

# DL Photo Specifications

Photographs are a common rejection trigger.

- **Size:** 35mm × 45mm  
- **Background:** White only  
- **Facing:** Front-facing  
- **Ears:** Both visible  
- **Glasses:** Not allowed unless healthcarely prescribed  
- **Recency:** Within 6 months  
- **Quality:** No shadows or pixelation  
- **Quantity:** 2–4 copies

# Form 1A healthcare Certificate Rules

A doctor-signed Form 1A from a local clinic is no longer sufficient in most states.

The doctor must:
- Be registered on the Parivahan portal  
- Upload Form 1A digitally  
- Use their MCI registration number  

## Applies To

- Transport / HMV DL applications  
- Transport DL renewals  
- Personal DL renewals after age 40 (in many states)  

### Important

- **Form 1** → Self-declaration  
- **Form 1A** → Doctor-certified healthcare form

# Can You Apply Without Aadhaar?

Yes.

Aadhaar is **not mandatory** for DL application.

You can apply using:
- Any valid age proof  
- Any valid address proof  

Without Aadhaar:
- No OTP verification  
- More likely in-person verification  

---

# DL Documents for First-Time Applicants (Age 18)

Checklist for standard LMV / MCWG LL:

- **Age Proof:** Aadhaar or 10th marksheet  
- **Address Proof:** Aadhaar or utility bill  
- **Photographs:** 4 passport-size  
- **Form 1:** Online self-declaration  
- **Signature:** Upload required  

---

# Document Upload Requirements for Online DL Applications

Follow these constraints carefully.

- **File Size:** Max 200KB  
- **Format:** JPG safest; PDF accepted in some cases  
- **Document Completeness:** All 4 corners visible  
- **Text Legibility:** Must be clear  
- **Photo Upload:** Scan of physical photo, not selfie  
- **Lighting:** Use natural daylight  
- **No Watermarks / Annotations**

# FAQs

## Is a healthcare certificate mandatory for DL in all cases?

No.

**Form 1A** is mandatory for:
- Transport / HMV  
- Renewals after age 40 in many states  

For standard LMV or MCWG:
- **Form 1 only** is sufficient  

---

## Is rent agreement valid for address proof?

Only if it is a **Registered Rent Agreement**.

Notarised agreements are generally rejected.

---

## What address proof works after moving cities?

Best options:
- Updated Aadhaar  
- Recent utility bill (within 3 months)

---

## Can I get a DL without Aadhaar?

Yes.

The application is valid without Aadhaar, though processing may be slower.

---

## My name differs on Aadhaar and marksheet. What should I do?

Carry a name-bridge document such as:
- Marriage Certificate  
- Gazette Notification  

Without this, your application may be rejected.

---

## What are the photo specifications?

- 35mm × 45mm  
- White background  
- Front-facing  
- No spectacles  
- Within last 6 months  

---

## Originals or self-attested copies?

- Online stage → Scanned copies  
- RTO visit → Originals required  

Carry:
- Originals  
- Two sets of self-attested photocopies  

---

## Form 1A rules for transport renewals?

Doctor must:
- Be Parivahan registered  
- Upload digitally  
- Use MCI registration number  

Paper certificate from an unregistered doctor will usually be rejected.
