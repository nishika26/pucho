# NGOs Supporting Autistic Children in India

**Domain:** Autism Support  
**Audience:** Parents, Caregivers, Families  

## Overview

Several NGOs in India provide support for autistic children and their families through services such as:

- Developmental assessments
- Early intervention
- Speech therapy
- Occupational therapy
- Special education
- Vocational training
- Parent support
- Advocacy and awareness programs

These organisations help families access therapy, education, resources, and community support.

---

# NGO Directory

## Ummeed Child Development Center

**Location:** Mumbai  
**Website:** https://ummeed.org/

### Services
- Developmental assessments
- Medical consultations
- Therapy sessions
- Educational support
- Parent and caregiver training

### Overview
Ummeed Child Development Center supports children with developmental disabilities, including autism. The organisation uses a multidisciplinary approach involving doctors, therapists, and educators to provide holistic care. They also conduct training programs for families and professionals.

# How to Choose an NGO

Parents may consider the following when selecting support:

- Location and accessibility
- Therapy services offered
- Cost and funding support
- Parent involvement programs
- Specialisation in autism support

India has several NGOs supporting autistic children and their families through therapy, education, advocacy, and community support.
